About

LogJam is a GTK client for LiveJournal.com.

The latest release is 4.6.2, released 7 Jun 2011. Get it from the download page.

Visit the LogJam tour for screenshots and a summary of LogJam's functionality.

Users

Read LogJam's LiveJournal to stay up-to-date with LogJam news. Feel free to ask any questions you have there—the people who read it are generally nice and are generally willing to help with problems.
